# Research Ethics Compliance Helper

## What This Skill Does
Generates documentation for research ethics compliance: ethics board applications, participant consent forms, data handling protocols, and risk assessments for human-subjects research.

## Documents Generated

### Ethics Application
```
1. PROJECT TITLE
2. PRINCIPAL INVESTIGATOR AND TEAM
3. PROJECT SUMMARY (non-technical, 300 words)
4. PARTICIPANTS
   - Target population
   - Recruitment method
   - Inclusion/exclusion criteria
   - Number of participants and justification
   - Vulnerable populations? (minors, patients, students)
5. PROCEDURES
   - What participants will do
   - Duration of participation
   - Location
   - Recording (video/audio/motion capture)
6. RISKS AND MITIGATION
   - Physical risks (if using equipment/sensors)
   - Psychological risks
   - Privacy risks
   - Mitigation strategies for each
7. BENEFITS
   - To participants (if any)
   - To science/society
8. INFORMED CONSENT PROCESS
9. DATA MANAGEMENT
   - Collection method
   - Storage (encrypted, anonymized)
   - Retention period
   - Destruction method
   - GDPR/KVKK compliance
10. CONFLICT OF INTEREST DECLARATION
```

### Consent Form Template
```
INFORMED CONSENT FORM

Study Title: [Title]
Researcher: [Name, Institution, Contact]

PURPOSE: [Plain language, 2-3 sentences]
PROCEDURES: [What you will be asked to do]
DURATION: [Time commitment]
RISKS: [Plain language risks]
BENEFITS: [Direct benefits, if any]
CONFIDENTIALITY: [How data is protected]
VOLUNTARY PARTICIPATION: [Right to withdraw at any time without penalty]
CONTACT: [Researcher + ethics committee contact for complaints]

☐ I have read and understood this form
☐ I agree to participate voluntarily
☐ I consent to [video/audio/data] recording

Signature: _____________ Date: _____________
```
