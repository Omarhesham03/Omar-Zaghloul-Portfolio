# Social Media Content Planner
## What This Skill Does
Generates structured content calendars for any platform with post types, captions, hashtag sets, and timing optimization.
## Content Calendar Template
```
MONTH: [Month Year]
Platform: [Instagram / LinkedIn / Twitter / TikTok]
Brand: [Brand name]
Goal: [Awareness / Engagement / Leads / Sales]

| Date | Day | Post Type | Topic | Caption | Hashtags | Visual | Time |
|------|-----|-----------|-------|---------|----------|--------|------|
| [Date] | Mon | Carousel | [Topic] | [Caption draft] | [5-10 tags] | [Visual brief] | [Optimal time] |
```
## Content Pillars (define 3-5 per brand)
- Educational (40%): Tips, how-tos, industry insights
- Behind-the-scenes (20%): Process, team, culture
- Social proof (20%): Testimonials, case studies, results
- Promotional (15%): Services, offers, CTAs
- Community (5%): Reposts, collaborations, responses
## Platform-Specific Rules
- **Instagram**: Visual-first, 2200 char caption max, 30 hashtags max (use 10-15), Reels for reach
- **LinkedIn**: Professional tone, 3000 char posts, no hashtag spam (3-5 max), articles for thought leadership
- **Twitter/X**: Concise, thread format for depth, 2-3 hashtags max
- **TikTok**: Hook in first 2 seconds, trending sounds, 15-60 sec optimal
