# Pricing Strategy Advisor
## Pricing Models
| Model | Best For | Pros | Cons |
|-------|----------|------|------|
| Fixed project | Clear scope | Predictable for both sides | Risk if scope creeps |
| Hourly | Unclear scope | Flexible | Client anxiety, income ceiling |
| Retainer | Ongoing work | Predictable income | May underdeliver perceived value |
| Value-based | High-impact projects | Highest margins | Hard to justify, requires trust |
| Productized | Repeatable services | Scalable, easy to sell | Less flexibility |

## Pricing Calculation
```
COST-PLUS:
  Hours estimated × hourly rate × 1.3 (margin) = price

VALUE-BASED:
  Client's problem cost (annual) × 10-20% = your fee
  Example: Client loses $100K/year to manual processes
  Your automation saves 50% → $50K value → charge $5-10K

MARKET-RATE:
  Research 5+ competitors → position at [percentile] based on quality/experience
```
