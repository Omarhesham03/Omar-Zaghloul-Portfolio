# Sales Call Prep Agent
## Call Prep Template
```
CALL PREP: [Company Name]
=========================
Date/Time: [When]
Attendees: [Who from their side + titles]
Call Objective: [What you want to achieve]

COMPANY SNAPSHOT:
- Industry: [Vertical]
- Size: [Employees/Revenue]
- Website: [URL + quick assessment]
- Recent news: [Anything notable]

PAIN POINT HYPOTHESES:
1. [Based on industry/size/signals: "They likely struggle with..."]
2. [...]
3. [...]

DISCOVERY QUESTIONS:
1. "What's currently the biggest bottleneck in your [operations/sales/tech]?"
2. "How are you handling [pain point] today?"
3. "What would solving this mean for your team in the next 6 months?"
4. "Have you tried other solutions? What worked/didn't?"
5. "Who else is involved in this decision?"

QUALIFICATION CHECKLIST (BANT):
- [ ] Budget: Can they afford this?
- [ ] Authority: Am I talking to the decision maker?
- [ ] Need: Is the pain real and urgent?
- [ ] Timeline: When do they need this solved?

TALKING POINTS:
- Our relevant experience: [1-2 similar projects]
- Key differentiator: [What makes us the right fit]
- Proposed next step: [What you'll suggest at end of call]
```
