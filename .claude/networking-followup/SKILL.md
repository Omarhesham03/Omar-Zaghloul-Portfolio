# Networking Follow-Up Agent
## Templates by Context

### Post-Conference
```
Subject: Great meeting you at [Event]

Hi [Name],

It was great connecting at [Event] — I enjoyed our conversation about [specific topic you discussed].

[One sentence of value: resource, introduction offer, or relevant insight]

Would love to stay in touch. [Soft CTA: coffee chat / LinkedIn connection / share resource]

Best,
[Name]
```

### LinkedIn Connection
```
Hi [Name], we met at [Event/Context]. Enjoyed discussing [topic]. Looking forward to staying connected.
```

### Cold Networking (Mutual Interest)
```
Hi [Name], I came across your work on [specific topic] and found it really relevant to [your work]. [Specific question or value offer]. Would you be open to a brief chat?
```

## Rules
- Send within 48 hours of meeting
- Reference something specific from the interaction
- Offer value before asking for anything
- Keep under 100 words for initial follow-up
