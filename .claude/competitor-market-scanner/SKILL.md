# Competitor and Market Scanner

## What This Skill Does
Takes a vertical + region and produces a competitive analysis with pricing intelligence, technology insights, and positioning recommendations.

|---------|------|----------|--------------|------------|-------------------|
| [Name] | [Est. employees] | [Services] | [Fixed/hourly/retainer] | [Stack] | [What makes them different] |

PRICING INTELLIGENCE:
- Market average: [Range]
- Premium tier: [Range]
- Budget tier: [Range]
- Your current positioning: [Where you fall]
- Recommendation: [Where to position and why]

GAPS AND OPPORTUNITIES:
1. [Service gap no competitor addresses]

1. [Competitor with aggressive pricing]

2. [...]
