# Client Communication Log
## Log Entry Template
```
| Date | Channel | Participants | Topic | Decision/Action | Status |
|------|---------|-------------|-------|-----------------|--------|
| [Date] | [Email/Call/Slack] | [Names] | [Topic] | [What was decided] | [Pending/Confirmed/Superseded] |
```
## Rules
- Log every decision that affects scope, timeline, or budget
- Log every approval (design, milestone, deliverable)
- Log every change request with outcome
- Include direct quotes for critical approvals ("Client said: approved as-is")
- Date and channel are mandatory for every entry
- Review log before any dispute or scope discussion
