# Newsletter and Email Campaign Writer
## Single Email Template
```
SUBJECT: [A/B variant 1] | [A/B variant 2]
PREVIEW TEXT: [40-90 chars, complements subject]
FROM: [Name <email>]

BODY:
[Opening — personal, relevant hook]
[Value — insight, tip, story, resource]
[CTA — single, clear action]
[Sign-off — warm, brief]
[P.S. — secondary CTA or teaser for next email]
```
## Drip Sequence Structure
```
Email 1 (Day 0): Welcome + immediate value
Email 2 (Day 2): Educational content
Email 3 (Day 5): Case study or social proof
Email 4 (Day 7): Soft pitch
Email 5 (Day 10): Hard pitch + urgency
Email 6 (Day 14): Break-up or re-engagement
```
## Metrics to Track
- Open rate (target: >25%)
- Click rate (target: >3%)
- Unsubscribe rate (alarm if >1%)
- Reply rate (engagement signal)
