# Business Plan Generator
## Lean Business Plan Structure
```
1. EXECUTIVE SUMMARY (1 page)
   - What the business does
   - Target market
   - Revenue model
   - Funding needs (if any)
   - Key milestones

2. PROBLEM AND SOLUTION
   - Problem: [Specific pain point with evidence]
   - Solution: [How you solve it]
   - Unique value proposition: [Why you, not competitors]

3. MARKET ANALYSIS
   - TAM (Total Addressable Market)
   - SAM (Serviceable Available Market)
   - SOM (Serviceable Obtainable Market)
   - Market trends
   - Competitor landscape

4. BUSINESS MODEL
   - Revenue streams
   - Pricing strategy
   - Cost structure (fixed + variable)
   - Unit economics (CAC, LTV, margins)

5. GO-TO-MARKET STRATEGY
   - Customer acquisition channels
   - Sales process
   - Marketing plan
   - Partnerships

6. OPERATIONS
   - Team structure
   - Key processes
   - Technology stack
   - Vendors/suppliers

7. FINANCIAL PROJECTIONS (12-36 months)
   - Revenue forecast
   - Expense forecast
   - Break-even analysis
   - Cash flow projection

8. RISKS AND MITIGATION
   - Market risks
   - Operational risks
   - Financial risks

9. MILESTONES AND TIMELINE
   - 3-month goals
   - 6-month goals
   - 12-month goals
```
