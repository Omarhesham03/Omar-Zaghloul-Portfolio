# Academic Collaboration Email Drafter

## What This Skill Does
Generates professional academic outreach emails calibrated for different relationship types: cold outreach to PIs, co-authorship inquiries, conference networking follow-ups.

## Templates

### Cold Outreach to PI
```
Subject: [Specific shared research interest] — Potential Collaboration

Dear Prof. [Name],

I am [Name], a [position] at [Institution] working on [specific topic].

I read your recent paper "[Title]" in [Venue] with great interest, particularly [specific aspect]. My own work on [your topic] has produced [brief result] that I believe complements your findings on [their specific finding].

I would welcome the opportunity to discuss potential synergies between our research. Would you be available for a brief call in the coming weeks?

Best regards,
[Name]
[Position, Institution]
[Website/Scholar profile]
```

### Rules
- Always cite a specific paper of theirs — never generic flattery
- State what you bring, not just what you want
- Keep under 150 words for cold outreach
- Include your academic profile link
