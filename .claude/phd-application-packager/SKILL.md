# PhD Application Packager

## What This Skill Does
Generates and tailors PhD application documents for specific programs, ensuring alignment between research interests, program strengths, and faculty research.

## Documents

### Research Statement (1-2 pages)
```
Paragraph 1: Research vision — broad area + specific problem
Paragraph 2: Prior research experience — what you did, what you found
Paragraph 3: Proposed research — what you want to investigate in the PhD
Paragraph 4: Why this program — specific faculty, labs, resources
Paragraph 5: Long-term goals — academic career, contributions to field
```

### Statement of Purpose (1-2 pages)
```
Paragraph 1: Hook + motivation for the field
Paragraph 2-3: Academic and research background (narrative, not CV rehash)
Paragraph 4: Research interests and potential directions
Paragraph 5: Why this specific program + faculty alignment
Paragraph 6: Career goals
```

### Tailoring Checklist
- [ ] Mention 2-3 specific faculty members and their recent work
- [ ] Reference specific labs, centers, or resources
- [ ] Align your experience with program focus areas
- [ ] Show knowledge of the department's recent publications
- [ ] Adapt research proposal to complement (not duplicate) existing lab work
