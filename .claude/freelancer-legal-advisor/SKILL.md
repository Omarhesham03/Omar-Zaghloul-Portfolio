# Freelancer Tax and Legal Structure Advisor
## Entity Comparison Framework
| Factor | Sole Proprietor | LLC/Ltd | Corporation |
|--------|----------------|---------|-------------|
| Setup complexity | Low | Medium | High |
| Personal liability | Unlimited | Limited | Limited |
| Tax treatment | Personal income | Pass-through or corp | Corp tax + dividends |
| Administrative burden | Minimal | Moderate | High |
| Credibility | Lower | Higher | Highest |
| Cost to maintain | Low | Medium | High |

## Turkey-Specific (Şahıs Şirketi vs Limited Şirket)
```
ŞAHIS ŞİRKETİ (Sole Proprietorship):
- Minimum capital: None
- Tax: Progressive income tax (15-40%)
- SGK: Mandatory BAĞ-KUR enrollment
- Bookkeeping: Simple (işletme defteri) if below threshold
- Best for: Solo freelancers, low revenue

LİMİTED ŞİRKET (LLC):
- Minimum capital: 10,000 TL
- Tax: 25% corporate tax + dividend withholding
- SGK: Company director pays SGK
- Bookkeeping: Double-entry (bilanço)
- Best for: Higher revenue, multiple clients, credibility needs
```

**Disclaimer**: This is a comparative framework, not tax or legal advice. Consult a Turkish SMMM (Certified Public Accountant) for specific guidance.
