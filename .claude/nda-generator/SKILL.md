# NDA Generator
## Types
- **Mutual NDA**: Both parties share confidential info (partnerships, collaborations)
- **Unilateral NDA**: One party discloses, other receives (client sharing project details)

## Key Clauses
```
1. PARTIES
2. DEFINITION OF CONFIDENTIAL INFORMATION
   - Include: business plans, code, data, designs, financials, client lists
   - Exclude: public info, independently developed, legally obtained from third parties
3. OBLIGATIONS
   - Protect with reasonable care
   - Use only for stated purpose
   - Limit disclosure to need-to-know
4. DURATION
   - Agreement period: [1-3 years typical]
   - Survival: Obligations survive [2-5 years] after termination
5. RETURN/DESTRUCTION of materials upon termination
6. REMEDIES for breach (injunctive relief)
7. EXCEPTIONS: Required by law (with notice)
8. GOVERNING LAW: [Jurisdiction]
9. SIGNATURES AND DATE
```
**Disclaimer**: Generated NDAs are templates. Have a lawyer review before execution.
