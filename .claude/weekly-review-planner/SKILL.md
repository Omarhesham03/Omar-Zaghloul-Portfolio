# Weekly Review and Planning Agent
## Template
```
WEEKLY REVIEW — Week of [Date]
================================

COMPLETED:
✓ [Task/achievement]
✓ [Task/achievement]

NOT COMPLETED (carry forward?):
✗ [Task] — Reason: [why] — Action: [carry/drop/delegate]

WINS:
🏆 [Best outcome this week]

LESSONS:
📝 [What I learned or would do differently]

METRICS:
- Hours worked: [X]
- Revenue this week: [X]
- Key progress: [1-sentence status on top goal]

NEXT WEEK PRIORITIES (max 5):
1. [Priority — specific and measurable]
2. [Priority]
3. [Priority]

BLOCKED ON:
- [What's waiting on someone/something else]

ENERGY CHECK: [High / Medium / Low]
[If low: what needs to change?]
```
