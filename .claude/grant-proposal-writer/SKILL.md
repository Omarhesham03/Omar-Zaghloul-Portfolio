# Grant and Research Proposal Writer

## What This Skill Does
Generates structured research/grant proposals following standard funding body formats. Adaptable to TÜBİTAK, EU Horizon, NSF, and institutional research grants.

## Standard Proposal Structure
```
1. PROJECT SUMMARY / ABSTRACT (250 words)
2. PROBLEM STATEMENT AND MOTIVATION
   2.1 Current state of the field
   2.2 Gap / Need
   2.3 Why now
3. OBJECTIVES
   - Primary objective
   - Secondary objectives (2-3)
   - Measurable outcomes
4. LITERATURE REVIEW (brief, focused on gap)
5. METHODOLOGY
   5.1 Research design
   5.2 Data collection
   5.3 Analysis methods
   5.4 Validation strategy
6. WORK PLAN AND TIMELINE
   - Gantt chart or milestone table
   - Deliverables per phase
7. BUDGET AND JUSTIFICATION
   - Personnel, equipment, travel, consumables
   - Each line item justified
8. EXPECTED IMPACT
   - Scientific contribution
   - Societal / industrial impact
   - Dissemination plan (publications, conferences)
9. RISK ASSESSMENT
   - Technical risks + mitigation
   - Timeline risks + contingency
10. TEAM AND QUALIFICATIONS
    - PI background
    - Collaborators
    - Institutional resources
```
