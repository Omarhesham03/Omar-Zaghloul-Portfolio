# Retainer Agreement Generator
## Template
```
RETAINER AGREEMENT
==================
Client: [Name]
Effective: [Start date]
Duration: [Month-to-month / 3-month / 6-month / 12-month]
Monthly Fee: [Amount]

INCLUDED SERVICES:
- [X] hours of development/maintenance per month
- Bug fixes and security patches
- [X] content updates per month
- Uptime monitoring ([X]% SLA)
- Monthly performance report

RESPONSE TIME SLA:
| Severity | Response Time | Resolution Target |
|----------|--------------|-------------------|
| Critical (site down) | 2 hours | 8 hours |
| High (feature broken) | 4 hours | 24 hours |
| Medium (minor issue) | 1 business day | 3 business days |
| Low (enhancement) | 3 business days | Next sprint |

EXCLUDED:
- New feature development (quoted separately)
- Third-party service outages
- Content creation

ROLLOVER: Unused hours [do / do not] roll over (max [X] hours banked)
TERMINATION: [30 days] written notice by either party
```
