# Academic CV Builder

## What This Skill Does
Generates academic CVs following conventions for faculty and researcher positions. Unlike industry resumes, academic CVs are comprehensive and can span multiple pages.

## Structure
```
[NAME]
[Title/Position]
[Department, Institution]
[Contact: email, phone, web, ORCID]

EDUCATION
  [Degree] — [Institution] — [Year]
  Thesis: [Title]
  Advisor: [Name]

ACADEMIC POSITIONS
  [Title] — [Institution] — [Dates]

RESEARCH INTERESTS
  [2-3 sentence summary of research areas]

PUBLICATIONS
  Refereed Journal Articles
    [N]. [Authors]. "[Title]." [Journal], vol. X, no. Y, pp. Z, Year.
  Conference Proceedings
    [N]. [Authors]. "[Title]." In Proc. [Conference], Year.
  Under Review / Preprints
    [N]. [description]

TEACHING EXPERIENCE
  [Course] — [Role] — [Institution] — [Semester]

GRANTS AND FUNDING
  [Grant title] — [Funder] — [Amount] — [Role] — [Dates]

AWARDS AND HONORS
  [Award] — [Granting body] — [Year]

PROFESSIONAL SERVICE
  Reviewer: [Journals/Conferences]
  Committee: [Service roles]

TECHNICAL SKILLS
  [Languages, frameworks, tools, hardware]

LANGUAGES
  [Language (Level)]

REFERENCES
  [Name, Title, Institution, Email] × 3
```
